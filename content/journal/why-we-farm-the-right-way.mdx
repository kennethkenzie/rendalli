Sustainable. Responsible. Ethical. We've all read the words. We've probably all stopped reading them.

So forget the words. Here's what it actually looks like at Rendalli.

## No hormones. No shortcuts.

Our fish grow on their own clock. It takes longer. The meat is firmer. The flavour is better. We believe you can taste the difference — and more importantly, that our children can grow up on food we'd feed our own.

## Water we can drink

We test the water daily. Not because a regulator tells us to, but because the lake is our office, our larder, and our inheritance.

- Dissolved oxygen logged every 4 hours
- Zero antibiotic use in 18 months and counting
- Community-led lakeshore cleanups every quarter

## People before product

Our farm employs 34 people from three neighbouring villages. Every wage is above the regional median. Every role has a training path. This is what "family-oriented" means to us — beyond the marketing deck.

> We feed families. Parents, children, grandparents.

When you buy a Rendalli fish, you're not just getting dinner. You're voting for a way of farming that respects the water it came from.
